Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This application (“the App”) is developed by Shivam Kamboj for educational and research purposes at UC Merced. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

The App requests only the minimum data required for secure Google login.

OAuth Scopes Requested

  • openid – used to verify your identity.
  • email (userinfo.email) – used to verify you as an authorized user.

No other Google data is accessed. The App does not access Drive, Contacts, Calendar, or any sensitive or restricted scopes.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your email is used solely for:

  • authentication,
  • verifying authorized user access,
  • maintaining a secure login session.

Your data is never used for advertising, sold to third parties, or analyzed.

3. Data Storage

The App does not store personal data except:

  • your email may temporarily appear in server authentication logs.

Logs follow normal hosting provider retention and are automatically deleted.

4. Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with any third-party companies, advertisers, or analytics services.

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your email access at any time:

Email: shivam1kamboj@gmail.com

6. Security

  • HTTPS enforced
  • IAP (Identity-Aware Proxy) protection
  • Least-privilege OAuth scopes
  • No long-term storage of OAuth tokens

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for university instructors and authorized testers. It is not designed for children under 13. No data about minors is collected.

8. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated to comply with Google OAuth and institutional requirements.
